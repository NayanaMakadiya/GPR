About
Government polytechnic Rajkot was established in the month of June 1964 by the government of Gujarat with an initial intake of 60 students in the civil engineering Diploma Course. This Polytechnic initially located at Rajkot. The new and fully equipped building for the Polytechnic was constructed later in the Year 1966, on a hillock near Aji Dam, a green lush surrounding ideal for the academics. This place is about 6Km. away from the Railway station.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering of this Institute started in 1964 with the inception of this polytechnic with an intake capacity of 30 students.

Mechanical Engineering

After establishment of Government Polytechnic in 1964. Mechanical Engineering Department comes in to existence in 1966, with initial intake of 30 students per year with semester system.

1966

Electrical Engineering

The department started in the year 1966 with the intake capacity of 60 students and now at this juncture the intake capacity has been increased to 240.

Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineering course was introduced to this polytechnic in the year 1988 with initial intake of 30 students per year.

2007

Information technology

The Department of Information Technology is established in year 2007 with intake of 60. Present intake is 180. The department has got a young dedicated staff who are always striving for excellence.
